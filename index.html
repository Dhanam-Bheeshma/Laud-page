Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as freedom,humanity, and revolution.He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism,including religious,caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrulwrote short stories,novels and essays but is best known for his songs and poems.He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals.
